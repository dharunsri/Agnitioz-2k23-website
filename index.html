About The Department & Hackathon

The department offers under graduate program (B.E) in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) was commenced in the year 2007 with an intake of 60 and post graduate program (M.E) in Computer Science and Engineering was commenced in the year 2012 with an intake of18 and the year of 2021 with intake of 06. The permanent affiliation has been received for B.E (CSE) from Anna University, Chennai from 2017-18 academic years onwards. The department has been accredited by NBA during the year 2019 and has been futhur re-accredited during the year 2022. CSE department has produced 21 UG rank holders and 12 PG rank holders. The department has obtained a Copyright on the title “Mobile Application for Mark Analysis” through IPR, India. So far students have been placed in various MNCs such as Cognizant Technology Solutions, Wipro Limited, HCL Technologies Limited, Infosys Limited, and Accenture. The department offers placement at an average of 100% of students every academic year. To enhance the practical exposure to students, Centre of Excellence lab in Data Science and Big data Analytics has been established with in association with ICT Academy and DELL EMC. CCNA certification course (in association with CISCO) is conducted regularly in the department for the welfare of the students. The department runs a students’ association, CELESTA, (Computer Engineers Leadership Empowerment Society and Technical Association) with the aim to conduct workshops, seminars, national and international conferences. The department also runs students’ chapter of Computer Society of India (CSI) through which various programs like workshops, guest lectures and seminars are organized on recent trends to enhance their technical knowledge. The department has signed seven MoUs with Industries and academic institutions like ICAR-National Research Centre for Banana (NRCB), Trichy, Tamil Nadu, Astonish InfoTech Pvt Ltd, Tech-Fi Technologies, DELL EMC and CISCO and Jyothi Institute of Technology, Bangalore, Wilma Communications, Chennai, Meiipourl Solutions Private Limited, Macnus Solutions Pvt Ltd.

Hack Themes

Problem statements should be based on following Themes.

Intelligent Automation

Intelligent automation refers to the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and robotic process automation (RPA) technologies to automate tasks and processes.

Green Technology

Green technology, also known as clean technology, refers to the development and application of environmentally friendly and sustainable products, processes, and systems.

Disaster Management

Disaster management refers to the processes and strategies aimed at preparing for, responding to, and recovering from natural or man-made disasters. The goal of disaster management is to save lives, reduce harm, and ensure a prompt and effective recovery following a disaster.

Smart Drone & Robots

Smart drones and robots refer to unmanned aerial and ground vehicles that are equipped with advanced sensors, cameras, and artificial intelligence algorithms. These devices are designed to perform complex tasks such as aerial photography, surveying, delivery, inspection, and more with a high degree of autonomy and accuracy.

Health Technology

Health technology refers to the use of technology to improve the delivery and quality of healthcare services. This includes a wide range of tools and devices, such as electronic health records (EHRs), telemedicine, wearable devices, medical devices, and more.

Security Surveillance

Security surveillance refers to the use of technology, such as cameras, sensors, and software, to monitor and protect physical and digital assets. It aims to detect, deter, and respond to security incidents, such as theft, vandalism, or unauthorized access. The goal of security surveillance is to provide a secure environment and to ensure the safety of people and assets.

Game Development

Game development is the process of designing, programming, and producing video games. It involves creating the concept, story, characters, and gameplay mechanics, as well as developing the software and tools needed to run the game.

Waste To Wealth

Waste to Wealth refers to the concept of converting waste materials into valuable resources, such as energy, fuel, or new products. This approach can help to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, conserve resources, and create new business opportunities, while also addressing the growing global waste management challenge.
